# Category 2: Conceptual & Chemical Laboratories
These biomes explore concepts of science, biology, and human experimentation.

**7. Transmutation Tide**
* **Theme:** Alchemy, potions, Philosopher's Stone.
* **Identity:** The water behaves like a boiling potion in a magical flask. Alchemical symbols float in the air. The fish here consist of a fusion of base metals that evolve into pure gold when pulled out.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ffd700", "#9c27b0"]` (Pure Gold & Mystic Purple).

**8. Steeped Springs**
* **Theme:** Tea, coffee, caffeine, boiling herbs.
* **Identity:** Colossal hot springs where the steam smells of matcha and herbal tea leaves. The fishes appear extremely hyperactive due to the naturally caffeine-dense environment.
* **Color Palette:** `["#33691e", "#aed581"]` (Dark & Light Matcha Green).

# Category 3: Art, Canvas, and Aesthetics
These biomes transform art forms into liquid ecosystems.

**9. Pigment Peninsula**
* **Theme:** Wet paint, canvas, oil painting.
* **Identity:** An ocean of acrylic and oil paint that never dries. Water ripples look like a painter's brushstrokes, and swimming creatures constantly leave trails of primary color swirls behind them.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ff007f", "#00e5ff"]` (Deep Magenta & Cyan Blue).

**10. Porcelain Ponds**
* **Theme:** Fragile ceramics, pottery, Kintsugi art.
* **Identity:** The water and land appear solid and smooth like a Ming Dynasty vase. However, gold cracks (Kintsugi) split the landscape, where fragile porcelain fishes hide.
* **Color Palette:** `["#fafafa", "#ffb300"]` (Porcelain White & Gold Veins).

**11. Celluloid Cenote**
* **Theme:** Cinematography, vintage film, black-and-white frames.
* **Identity:** As soon as you arrive here, your entire visual turns to Sepia. The sound of crickets is replaced by the whirring of a projector tape. Fish here move at a framerate of 12 frames per second, mimicking 1920s films.
* **Color Palette:** `["#705a45", "#d6c4a8"]` (Antique Sepia & Film Gray).

**12. Stained-Glass Sanctuary**
* **Theme:** Gothic church windows, mosaic shards, refracted light.
* **Identity:** The ocean floor is formed by giant multi-colored glass shards. When fishing here, the fish sound like glass fragments rubbing against each other as they fight the rod's pull.
* **Color Palette:** `["#b71c1c", "#01579b"]` (Ruby Red & Cobalt Blue).

# Category 4: Surrealism & Dimensional Anomalies
The highest rarity biomes that encompass psychological concepts and bizarre dimensions.

**13. Somnambulist Shallows**
* **Theme:** Dreams, subconscious, hallucinations.
* **Identity:** Here, gravity twists, clocks melt like a Dali painting, and doors open to nothingness. The creatures fished up are manifestations of fears or fond memories from the player's subconscious mind.
* **Color Palette:** `["#4a148c", "#b388ff"]` (Deep Sleep Purple & Soft Violet).

**14. Tabletop Trench**
* **Theme:** Board games, casinos, RNG (Luck).
* **Identity:** The ocean floor is covered in green casino felt. Water bubbles are shaped like octagonal dice. This is a meta biome; catch rates fluctuate drastically based on imaginary "dice" rolls by the server (system).
* **Color Palette:** `["#1b5e20", "#f44336"]` (Casino Green & Roulette Red).

**15. Silhouette Strait**
* **Theme:** 2D Shadows, absolute contrast.
* **Identity:** A two-dimensional (2D) realm where depth has no meaning. The entire background glows blindingly white, while the water and fish are merely dense black shadows that intersect geometrically.
* **Color Palette:** `["#000000", "#ffffff"]` (Absolute Black & Absolute White).

**16. Typography Trench**
* **Theme:** Typography fonts, letters, ocean of characters.
* **Identity:** An ink ocean filled with coral reefs formed from grammatical symbols (commas, periods). The species fished here are not actual fish, but rather ancient Words and Sentences jumping out of the water.
* **Color Palette:** `["#263238", "#cfd8dc"]` (Ink Gray & Bright Paper).

**17. Broth Basin**
* **Theme:** Culinary, boiling soup, spices.
* **Identity:** Going against the norm of serious biomes, the water here is a thick, boiling broth. Giant star anise serve as islands, and the clouds consist of spice steam. Your main prey are savory-flavored fish.
* **Color Palette:** `["#d84315", "#ffcc80"]` (Chili Oil Red & Noodle Brown).

**18. Cartographer's Cove**
* **Theme:** Geographic maps, compasses, latitude/longitude lines.
* **Identity:** You fish on a giant, living paper map. Topographical grid lines float on the surface. Players don't catch actual fish, but rather the 'Sea Monster Illustrations' often drawn at the edges of the maps during the age of exploration.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ffecb3", "#bcaaa4"]` (Parchment Yellow & Ink Brown).

**19. Aromatic Archipelago**
* **Theme:** Perfumes, floral essences, floating glass bottles.
* **Identity:** This ocean consists of pure essential oils. Thick vapors carrying the scent of lavender and roses drift into the sky. Fish here are translucent and possess flower petal scales that emit a hypnotic fragrance when caught.
* **Color Palette:** `["#f8bbd0", "#e1bee7"]` (Rose Pink & Lavender Purple).

**20. Ectoplasmic Eddy**
* **Theme:** Ghosts, spirit world, supernatural chill.
* **Identity:** There is no water at all; the pool is filled with bone-chilling ectoplasmic vapor. The creatures here are spirits and echoes of ancient sea life that haven't moved on to the afterlife. The minigame screen is sometimes interrupted by supernatural glitches.
* **Color Palette:** `["#84ffff", "#1de9b6"]` (Haunted Cyan & Spectral Green).

# Category 5: Macro-Botany & Extreme Horticulture
These biomes shrink the player down to explore the extreme, dangerous, and bizarre aspects of plant life and horticulture.

**21. Carnivorous Canopy**
* **Theme:** Pitcher plants, digestive acids, Venus flytraps.
* **Identity:** You are fishing inside the colossal belly of a pitcher plant. The "water" is a pool of thick, glowing digestive enzymes. The fish here are acid-resistant extremophiles that feed on dissolved insects.
* **Color Palette:** `["#b71c1c", "#33691e"]` (Digestive Crimson & Acid Green).

**22. Pollen Ponds**
* **Theme:** Allergy-inducing dust, floating spores, stamen forests.
* **Identity:** The water is entirely replaced by a dense, fluid-like sea of golden pollen. Every cast creates a massive cloud of dust. The creatures here swim through the air-light powder, making them extremely agile.
* **Color Palette:** `["#fbc02d", "#fff9c4"]` (Pollen Yellow & Dusty Cream).

**23. Nectar Nexus**
* **Theme:** Giant blooming flowers, sticky sweetness, ultraviolet trails.
* **Identity:** Located in the center of an impossibly large orchid. The water is a viscous, hyper-sweet nectar that glows under ultraviolet light. Fish are often stuck in the sap-like liquid, requiring immense rod strength to pull out.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ff4081", "#ea80fc"]` (Vibrant Magenta & Ultraviolet Pink).

**24. Petrified Peat-Bog**
* **Theme:** Compressed moss, methane bubbles, ancient roots.
* **Identity:** A claustrophobic, underground marsh made of compressed, ancient plant matter. The water bubbles violently with trapped methane gas. The fish are blind, ancient bottom-feeders covered in roots.
* **Color Palette:** `["#3e2723", "#1b5e20"]` (Peat Brown & Deep Swamp Green).

**25. Thorn-Thicket Trench**
* **Theme:** Sharp briars, blood-red roses, impenetrable defenses.
* **Identity:** A perilous aquatic maze where coral reefs are replaced by razor-sharp rose thorns. Your fishing line constantly risks being snagged or snapped. The fish here are heavily armored to survive their own habitat.
* **Color Palette:** `["#212121", "#880e4f"]` (Bark Black & Blood Rose Red).

# Category 6: Mathematical & Physics Anomalies
These biomes break the fundamental laws of reality, turning theoretical physics and complex geometry into liquid ecosystems.

**26. Mandelbrot Maelstrom**
* **Theme:** Infinite fractals, recursive loops, mathematical perfection.
* **Identity:** A hypnotic whirlpool where every wave is a smaller version of the whirlpool itself. As you reel in a fish, you realize the fish is made of smaller fish, zooming inward infinitely.
* **Color Palette:** `["#6200ea", "#00bfff"]` (Deep Indigo & Deep Sky Blue).

**27. Quantum Superposition Sea**
* **Theme:** Probability waves, Schrödinger's paradox, unobserved states.
* **Identity:** The water flickers in and out of existence. The fish here exist in multiple states and rarities simultaneously; their actual identity and weight are only determined the exact second you successfully catch them.
* **Color Palette:** `["#000000", "#ffffff"]` (Void Black & Flash White).

**28. Möbius Strip Stream**
* **Theme:** Non-orientable surfaces, infinite topological loops.
* **Identity:** A river with no top, bottom, beginning, or end. If you cast your line too far, the hook might come up from right behind you. The fish swim upside down and right side up at the same time.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ff5722", "#607d8b"]` (Deep Orange & Blue Grey).

**29. Fibonacci Floodplain**
* **Theme:** The Golden Ratio, perfect spirals, nautilus shells.
* **Identity:** Every ripple in the water perfectly follows the Fibonacci sequence. The fish here possess mathematically perfect anatomical symmetry, moving in mesmerizing, calculated spiral patterns.
* **Color Palette:** `["#ffd54f", "#5d4037"]` (Golden Ratio Yellow & Nautilus Brown).

**30. Tesseract Trench**
* **Theme:** 4D hypercubes, spatial folding, intersecting dimensions.
* **Identity:** A four-dimensional body of water projected into a 3D space. You cast your line into the water, but the bobber appears floating in the sky. The fish move through unseen geometric angles.
* **Color Palette:** `["#00e676", "#1a237e"]` (Hyper Green & Navy Space).

# Category 7: The Giant's Domestic Domain
Taking inspiration from macro-photography, these biomes turn forgotten everyday household environments into vast, treacherous oceans.

**31. Bathtub Billows**
* **Theme:** Soap suds, ceramic tiles, rubber ducks.
* **Identity:** An endless ocean of foam and iridescent soap bubbles enclosed by towering white ceramic cliffs. The ecosystem relies on artificial bath bombs, and the fish hide beneath massive rubber ducks.
* **Color Palette:** `["#e0f7fa", "#ffffff"]` (Soap Bubble Cyan & Foam White).

**32. Attic Dust-Sea**
* **Theme:** Dust bunnies, cobwebs, forgotten heirlooms.
* **Identity:** A dry, gray ocean made entirely of shifting dust bunnies and thick spiderwebs. Sunlight pierces through the darkness in distinct, dusty rays. You fish for moth-eaten creatures and forgotten memories.
* **Color Palette:** `["#9e9e9e", "#795548"]` (Dust Grey & Old Wood Brown).

**33. Junk Drawer Delta**
* **Theme:** Tangled wires, old batteries, paperclips, lost keys.
* **Identity:** A chaotic, metallic swamp. The currents are magnetic fields generated by leaking AA batteries. You must carefully navigate your line through a dense forest of tangled charging cables and rusted house keys.
* **Color Palette:** `["#757575", "#ff9800"]` (Metal Key Grey & Battery Copper).

**34. Static-Carpet Shallows**
* **Theme:** Nylon fibers, static electricity, friction.
* **Identity:** A sprawling, neon-colored savanna made of towering carpet threads. Walking creates violent static electric storms. The fish here swim through the fibers and are charged with high-voltage static.
* **Color Palette:** `["#d32f2f", "#ffeb3b"]` (Carpet Red & Static Spark Yellow).

**35. Refrigerator Rill**
* **Theme:** Freon gas, artificial frost, towering leftovers.
* **Identity:** A brutally cold, artificially lit tundra. The rivers run with liquid freon coolant. Islands are formed from ancient, crystallized leftovers. The fish are uniquely adapted to survive in absolute artificial zero.
* **Color Palette:** `["#b2ebf2", "#c5e1a5"]` (Freon Blue & Mold Green).

# Category 8: Philosophical Paradigms
The absolute endgame biomes. You are no longer fishing in physical spaces, but casting your line into abstract human concepts, ethics, and emotions.

**36. Sea of Solipsism**
* **Theme:** Mirrors, self-reflection, the illusion of reality.
* **Identity:** A perfectly still, silver ocean that acts as a flawless mirror. There is no background, only the reflection of the player. The fish you catch here are merely fragmented reflections of your own avatar.
* **Color Palette:** `["#eceff1", "#b0bec5"]` (Mirror Silver & Reflective Chrome).

**37. Utilitarian Utopia**
* **Theme:** Absolute logic, sterile balance, maximum efficiency.
* **Identity:** A disturbingly perfect, sterile grid-world. There is no joy or chaos here, only calculated efficiency. The fish never fight the line; they mathematically calculate if escaping is worth the energy expenditure.
* **Color Palette:** `["#fafafa", "#90caf9"]` (Clinical White & Sterile Blue).

**38. Nihilist's Null**
* **Theme:** Absolute emptiness, apathy, grayscale meaninglessness.
* **Identity:** A featureless, infinitely gray void. There is no music, no weather, and no visual flair. The fish you catch have no eyes, no color, and offer zero resistance during the minigame. 
* **Color Palette:** `["#424242", "#212121"]` (Bleak Grey & Empty Black).

**39. Hedonist's Haven**
* **Theme:** Pure dopamine, sensory overload, extreme indulgence.
* **Identity:** A blindingly vibrant, overwhelmingly loud ocean of liquid dopamine. The colors shift so fast it induces vertigo. Catching fish here gives an extreme, temporary boost to your stats, but losing one heavily drains your screen's color.
* **Color Palette:** `["#d50000", "#aa00ff"]` (Passion Red & Ecstasy Purple).

**40. Ship of Theseus Shoals**
* **Theme:** The paradox of replacement, weathered wood, identity crisis.
* **Identity:** A swirling vortex of floating wooden planks, rusted nails, and torn sails. Every time you catch a fish here, it instantly transforms into a different species in your inventory, leaving you to wonder if it's still the same fish you hooked.
* **Color Palette:** `["#8d6e63", "#546e7a"]` (Weathered Wood & Rusted Iron).
